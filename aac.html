Algonquin Accounting Club

Logo Design

Objective

The objective of this project is to create a logo for Algonquin’s accounting club that represents their professional attitude, values, and goals of their people.

Theme

This logo shows the initialism of Algonquin Accounting Club – AAC. The logo as a whole depicts a line graph through the initialism. The graph shows a positive incline, which represents the club’s growing potential and success. The combination of blue and green express harmony, intelligence, and trustworthiness within the organization.
