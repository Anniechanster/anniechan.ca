Campaign

Ottawa Tourism

Ottawa Tourism is a destination marketing organization that promotes Ottawa and Canada's Capital Region to tourists, tour operators and meeting planners. Thier main goal is to promote the destination to increase the number of visits and to strengthen its economic impact.

Purpose

At Blackiron Agency, concepts were developed for Ottawa Tourism with the focus on sport services. The goal of this campaign was to bring in major provincial, national and international sporting events to Ottawa. As Canada's capital city and a host to outstanding sport venues, Ottawa would be a great place for athletes.
