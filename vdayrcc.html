Valentine's Day at RCC

Holiday Promotion

Rideau Carleton Casino

An entertainment destination in Ottawa located on Albion Road. The casino has over 1,200 slot machines, live table games, live harness racing, and multiple on-site restaurants.

Objective

The goal of this project was to have Blackiron Agency create an overall theme to promote Valentine's day activities at Rideau Carelton Casino. Since the casino is transitioning to the Future Home of Hard Rock Ottawa, a heart-shaped guitar was chosen to represent the music and contentment of the casino and the holiday. Activities included a Valentine's Day dinner at The Clubhouse and chances to win prizes such as a romantic weekend getaway and Pandora gift cards when playing slot games.
