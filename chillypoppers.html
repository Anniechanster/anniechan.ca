Chilly Poppers

T-shirt Design

Objective

A fun logo and T-shirt design created for an ultimate frisbee team in Ottawa. Chilly Poppers came from a play on words that revolve around a ultimate frisbee term, 'popper'. Using this pun, a playful logo was created, representing a cold, but cool chilly pepper who can pop!

Style

The pepper was also used at the back of the T-shirt for a subtle pop of colour and reference.
