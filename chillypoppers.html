Chilly Poppers

T-shirt Design

Objective

A fun logo and T-shirt design created for an ultimate team in Ottawa.

Style

This piece was inspired by an artist named, Charley Harper. He was well-known for his highly stylized wildlife illustrations. Harper captured the essence of his subjects with the fewest visual elements possible. Similarly, in this pieace, a Puffin is illustrated catching their prey in their natural habitat. Using only blocks of shape and continuous lines, a gestault piece was created.
